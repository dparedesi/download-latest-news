March 16, 2026 05:56 ET | Source: Novo Nordisk A/S Novo Nordisk A/S Bagsværd, Denmark, 16 March 2026 – On 4 February 2026, Novo Nordisk initiated a share repurchase programme in accordance with Article 5 of Regulation No 596/2014 of the European Parliament and Council of 16 April 2014 (MAR) and the Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2016/1052 of 8 March 2016 (the "Safe Harbour Rules"). This programme is part of the overall share repurchase programme of up to DKK 15 billion to be executed during a 12-month period beginning 4 February 2026. Under the programme initiated 4 February 2026, Novo Nordisk will repurchase B shares for an amount up to DKK 3.8 billion in the period from 4 February 2026 to 4 May 2026. The total amount of A and B shares in the company is 4,465,000,000 including treasury shares. Novo Nordisk expects to repurchase B shares for an amount up to DKK 15 billion during a 12-month period beginning 4 February 2026. As of 13 March 2026, Novo Nordisk has since 4 February 2026 repurchased a total 6,577,992 B shares at an average share price of DKK 271.14 per B share equal to a transaction value of DKK 1,783,540,174.
