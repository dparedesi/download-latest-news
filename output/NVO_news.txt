Title: Novo Nordisk A/S - share repurchase programme

April 27, 2026 04:56 ET | Source: Novo Nordisk A/S Novo Nordisk A/S Bagsværd, Denmark, 27 April 2026 – On 4 February 2026, Novo Nordisk initiated a share repurchase programme in accordance with Article 5 of Regulation No 596/2014 of the European Parliament and Council of 16 April 2014 (MAR) and the Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2016/1052 of 8 March 2016 (the "Safe Harbour Rules"). This programme is part of the overall share repurchase programme of up to DKK 15 billion to be executed during a 12-month period beginning 4 February 2026. Under the programme initiated 4 February 2026, Novo Nordisk will repurchase B shares for an amount up to DKK 3.8 billion in the period from 4 February 2026 to 4 May 2026. Since the announcement 20 April 2026, the following transactions have been made: The details for each transaction made under the share repurchase programme are published on novonordisk.com. With the transactions stated above, Novo Nordisk owns a total of 30,797,791 B shares of DKK 0.10 as treasury shares, corresponding to 0.7% of the share capital. The total amount of A and B shares in the company is 4,465,000,000 including treasury shares. Novo Nordisk expects to repurchase B shares for an amount up to DKK 15 billion during a 12-month period beginning 4 February 2026. As of 24 April 2026, Novo Nordisk has since 4 February 2026 repurchased a total 13,407,992 B shares at an average share price of DKK 256.48 per B share equal to a transaction value of DKK 3,438,931,195. Novo Nordisk is a leading global healthcare company founded in 1923 and headquartered in Denmark.
