Title: Reliance Power shares down 31% in 1 month. Should you buy the dip or steer clear?
URL: https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/markets/stocks/news/reliance-power-shares-down-31-in-1-month-should-you-buy-the-dip-or-steer-clear/articleshow/123133023.cms
Time Published: 2025-08-06T04:46:17Z
Full Content:
Reliance Power shares have plummeted 31% in a month amid regulatory scrutiny and Anil Ambani's ED questioning over alleged loan fraud. Technical indicators signal a bearish trend, with the stock trading below key moving averages. Analysts advise caution, suggesting investors avoid new entries and consider exiting existing positions until stability is confirmed, given the ongoing investigations and reputational risks.
