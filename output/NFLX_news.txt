Smart money is deploying capital in entirely different ways. Trillion-dollar technology conglomerates exercise strict discipline, prioritizing margin defense over legacy studio bailouts. Content production is highly commoditized and incredibly capital-intensive. Distribution infrastructure operates as a high-margin digital tollbooth. Netflix lists Alphabet, Amazon, Apple, Comcast, Disney, Meta, Roblox, TikTok, and local media among rivals, and content amortization growth is front-half-weighted in 2026, which can pressure near-term margins.
