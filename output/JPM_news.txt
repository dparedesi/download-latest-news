This on-ground exposure enabled a deeper understanding of patient-specific calibration, clinical fitting and rehabilitation. Sessions progressed from theory to application, covering casting techniques, lamination, fitting trials and functional testing, alongside app-based tuning and patient training simulations that reflect the growing integration of digital tools in prosthetic care. Through integrated landfill reclamation and advanced material recovery processes, legacy waste is excavated, stabilised, segregated, and converted into reusable resources.
