Title: US stock market crashes — Dow, S&P 500, Nasdaq all lost after shocking August jobs report; possible Fed rate cut ahead
URL: https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/international/us/us-stock-market-crashes-dow-sp-500-nasdaq-all-lost-after-shocking-august-jobs-report-possible-fed-rate-cut-ahead/articleshow/123722243.cms
Time Published: 2025-09-05T15:12:42Z
Full Content:
U.S. stocks swung sharply lower Friday after a surprisingly weak August jobs report reinforced the case for a Federal Reserve rate cut at its September 17 meeting. Stocks initially surged on the prospect of cheaper borrowing costs but quickly reversed as investors digested the economic slowdown behind the Fed’s likely pivot.
