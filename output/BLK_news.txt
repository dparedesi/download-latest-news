Jack Bogle became a legend by popularizing low-cost passive index funds at the Vanguard Group, which he founded in 1975 after spending the first 23 years of his career at Wellington Management. ‚ÄúDon‚Äôt look for the needle in the haystack. In an interview with Forbes in June 2017, a year and a half before his death at 89, Bogle wasn‚Äôt giving any ground. ‚ÄúThe idea of making money for the funds‚Äô shareholders is counter-opposed to the idea of making money for fund managers,‚Äù he declared.
