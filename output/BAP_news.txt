C/O BANCO DE CREDITO Lima, Oct. 25, 2024 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Lima, PERU, October 25th, 2024 – Credicorp Ltd. announces to its shareholders and the market that its 3Q24 Earnings Release Report will be released on Thursday, November 7th, 2024, after market close. Credicorp’s Webcast / Conference Call to discuss such results; will be held on Friday, November 8th, 2024, at 9:30 a.m. ET (9:30 a.m. Lima, Peru time). The call will be hosted by: Gianfranco Ferrari – Chief Executive Officer, - Alejandro Perez Reyes - Chief Financial Officer, Francesca Raffo – Chief Innovation Officer, Cesar Rios - Chief Risk Officer, Diego Cavero – Head of Universal Banking, Cesar Rivera - Head of Insurance and Pensions, Carlos Sotelo - Mibanco CFO and Investor Relations Team. About Credicorp Credicorp Ltd. (NYSE: BAP) is the leading financial services holding company in Peru with presence in Chile, Colombia and Bolivia. Credicorp has a diversified business portfolio organized into four lines of business: Universal Banking, through Banco de Credito del Peru – BCP and Banco de Credito de Bolivia; Microfinance, through Mibanco in Peru and Colombia; Insurance & Pension Funds, through Grupo Pacifico and Prima AFP; and Investment Management & Advisory, through Credicorp Capital, Wealth Management at BCP and Atlantic Security Bank.
