Q2 operating cash flow was $2.165 billion against capex of just $58 million, on top of a record $10.030 billion in FY2025 operating cash flow. Moat monetizing AI. Subscription revenue reached $6.39 billion, up 14% year over year. What does not change is that the stock now trades below where those insiders sold, the cash machine is unbroken, and the recurring revenue base keeps compounding regardless of who signs the 10-Q. Wall Street is paying a stagnant-business multiple for a franchise generating 35.3% operating margins and tripling its AI revenue line.
